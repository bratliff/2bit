OBJECTIVE

Make great interactive experiences

Emergent technologies on the web introduce a host of new opportunities and challenges in web development. The landscape is changing rapidly with the rise of tablets and phones, creating a multitude of new ways in which the internet is accessed and used. Mobile presents new opportunities and challenges for web developers.

I employ responsive web design when possible to create a web product that can be accessed on any size device. Advances in Javascript, JQuery, CSS and HTML5 present a whole new quiver of arrows to create new and unique web products. It's an exciting time in front-end web development.

BACKGROUND

Solid experience in web design and development

My experience in the digital world is far ranging. As the web has evolved from the days of dial-up, I have worn a variety of hats, including front-end developer, interactive designer, UI architect, news producer, writer and editor. I have worked for online portals, media start-ups, advertising agencies, financial firms, not-for-profits, real estate developers and a variety of other businesses large and small. Most recently, I have worked primarily for advertising agencies Saatchi and Saatchi Health and KBS+P / Spies & Assassins.

In my experience, the technologies change but the fundamentals do not. The principles of good and intuitive design are the bedrock of worthwhile online experiences. As a developer, I am always thinking about design. As a designer, I leverage my programming knowledge to create a design that makes sense.

Recently, I have taken courses and / or conducted independent study into new frameworks and tools that are helping web developers unleash the web's new potential. Of particular interest to me is the growing amount of action on the front-end using JavaScript MVC frameworks like Backbone and Ember and also Knockout (technically MVVM). The JQuery Mobile library offers a great suite of tools to accelerate mobile development and Bootstrap and Less are excellent products to facilitate truly responsive design as that becomes increasingly important to clients and companies. In addition, I have taken classes in the Python programming language, Node.js and MongoDB.
